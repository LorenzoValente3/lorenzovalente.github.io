Lorenzo Valente

The physical sciences are my main area of interest. As part of my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in physics, I have gained a deep understanding of the working principle of Radiation Detectors in High Energy Physics. My interests include Particle Physics, Software and Computing for High Energy Physics, Statistical Data Analysis and Electronic devices used in these fields, specifically Field Programmable Gate Arrays. Additionally, I am particularly passionate about the Machine and Deep Learning techniques emerging in recent years and their applications in physics. Besides these, I also studied Classical Piano.
